A long-form conversation exploring how people make meaning in a world that measures success through money, status, and scale.

Question Everything is Socratic by design — we ask, we don't answer. We speak with people about their deliberate choices, exploring the true intent behind their decisions and the tradeoffs that followed. Not heroes. Not highlight reels. Real people examining what a well-lived life actually looks like.

If you sense that the dominant metrics are incomplete, this show is for you.

Be a Guest

We're looking for people who have thought carefully about the choices they've made — and the ones they didn't. Whether you walked away from something, built something quietly, or are still in the middle of figuring it out, we want to hear about the tradeoffs, the turning points, and what you've learned about what actually matters.

We're not looking for polished success stories. We're looking for honest ones.
